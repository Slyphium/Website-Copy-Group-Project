Select a lead

At TinyHorse, a "lead" refers to the piece the human clips into a connecting ring to guide the dogs. Our options include hand-held leads and wearable leads that allow you to be hands-free as well as provide hands-on control when necessary.

Use one lead or combine multiple leads to split your group of dogs into smaller, more manageable clusters. For example, the Handler has a second clip in the handle so it can be attached to one of the rings on the Leader . Increasing the number of leads at your disposal increases the options you have for managing and balancing your group of dogs.
